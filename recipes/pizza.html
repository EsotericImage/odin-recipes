Pizza

The best pizza around town!

Lazy day? Can't decide what else to eat? Everyone loves a good pizza!

Any cheese pizza lover will love this quick scratch to stomach recipe

Ingredients

Dough Preparations:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp olive oil additional

Time for toppings!

  • 3 tbsp softened unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves medium galic minced
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese shredded
  • 1 cup white cheddar cheese shredded
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese shredded
  • 1/4 cup Parmesean cheese freshley grated
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley optional
